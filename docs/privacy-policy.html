1. Who We Are

Surve is a marketplace that connects social-media content creators (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and similar platforms) with hospitality venues — hotels, restaurants, bars, and resorts — for mutually beneficial booking arrangements. The platform facilitates discovery, negotiation, payment, and review of those arrangements.

Data controller: Surve, Inc. (or the legal entity operating Surve).
Contact: privacy@surve.app

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account & Profile Data

  • Full name, email address, and password (hashed — we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Profile photo and biography
  • User role: Creator or Business
  • For creators: linked social handles, follower/subscriber counts, niche categories, engagement statistics, platform badges
  • For businesses: venue name, address, venue type, website, and representative contact details

2.2 Booking & Transaction Data

  • Listing details created by businesses (title, description, deliverables, compensation, requirements)
  • Booking applications and status history (pending, active, completed, cancelled)
  • Booking dates, agreed compensation, and any notes exchanged during the booking
  • Payout records and transaction history stored in our platform

2.3 Messages

Content of in-app conversations between creators and business representatives. Messages are stored to provide the messaging feature and to enable dispute resolution.

2.4 Reviews & Ratings

Written reviews and star ratings left by creators or businesses after a completed booking.

2.5 Payment Information

We do not store raw card numbers, CVVs, or full bank account details. Payment data is handled by Stripe (see Section 4). We store only Stripe-issued tokens, the last four digits of a card, card brand, and expiry month/year for display purposes.

2.6 Device & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • IP address and approximate geographic location derived from it
  • App session events, screen views, and feature interactions (for diagnostics and product improvement)
  • Crash reports and error logs via Sentry

2.7 Media

Photos uploaded by users for profile pictures or listing images. Images are stored in Supabase Storage and served via CDN. We do not scan media for advertising purposes.

2.8 Notification Preferences

Your push-notification opt-in status, Expo push token, and per-category notification toggles (booking updates, messages, promotions).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the service: Match creators with venues, process bookings, route messages, and display public profiles.
  • Process payments: Initiate Stripe payment intents, hold platform fees (5%), and facilitate payouts.
  • Personalization: Surface relevant listings and creators based on your niche, location, and activity.
  • Trust & safety: Detect fraud, resolve disputes, enforce our Terms of Service, and investigate abuse reports.
  • Communications: Send transactional emails (booking confirmations, payout receipts) and, where you have opted in, marketing updates.
  • Push notifications: Deliver booking status changes, new messages, and promotional offers (see Section 6).
  • Product improvement: Analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to prioritise features and fix bugs.
  • Legal obligations: Retain records as required by tax, financial, or other applicable law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use your data for targeted advertising via third-party ad networks.

4. Payments — Stripe

Surve uses Stripe, Inc. as its payment processor. When you add a payment method or complete a booking payment, your card details are collected directly by Stripe's SDK and transmitted to Stripe's servers; your raw card data never passes through Surve's servers.

  • Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.
  • We receive and store only a Stripe PaymentMethod ID, the last four digits, card brand, and expiry for display.
  • Stripe may collect additional fraud-prevention signals (device fingerprint, IP address) governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Payout information (bank account or debit card) for creators is entered directly into Stripe Connect's hosted onboarding; Surve does not store routing or account numbers.
  • A platform fee of 5% is retained by Surve on each completed booking transaction.

5. Infrastructure — Supabase

All application data — user records, listings, bookings, messages, reviews — is stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) project hosted on AWS infrastructure in a region selected for compliance and performance. Supabase Storage holds user-uploaded media.

  • Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).
  • Database access uses Row-Level Security (RLS) policies so that each user can only read and write their own records; business representatives can only access data for their own venue.
  • Supabase's data processing practices are governed by their Privacy Policy and a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) we maintain with them.
  • Supabase Edge Functions process server-side logic (e.g., creating Stripe payment intents) and have access only to the data required for that operation.

6. Push Notifications

Surve uses Expo Push Notifications (backed by Apple Push Notification Service on iOS and Firebase Cloud Messaging on Android) to deliver real-time alerts.

  • We collect and store an Expo push token associated with your account and device.
  • Notifications are sent for: new booking applications, booking status changes, new messages, payout processed, and (where enabled) promotional offers.
  • You can opt out of notification categories individually inside Profile → Preferences → Notifications, or revoke the permission entirely in your device's system settings. Revoking permission stops all notifications but does not affect your account.
  • We do not use your push token to identify you to third-party advertisers.

7. Creator Analytics & Stats

Creator profiles display publicly available social metrics such as follower count, average engagement rate, niche tags, and verified platform badges. These figures are:

  • Entered manually by the creator or synced when the creator chooses to connect a social account via OAuth.
  • Visible to any logged-in business user browsing the marketplace.
  • Not sold to data brokers or aggregated for resale.

Internal analytics (e.g., how many times a profile was viewed or a listing was opened) are used by Surve to surface trending creators and improve search ranking. Aggregated impression data may be shown to creators in their earnings dashboard. Raw viewer identities are not exposed to creators.

If you connect a social platform account, we request only the minimum scopes needed (typically read-only public profile data). You may disconnect linked accounts at any time from Profile → Settings → Connected Accounts.

8. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • With the other party to a booking: When you apply to or create a listing, the relevant profile details (name, photo, stats for creators; venue name and details for businesses) are shared with the counterpart.
  • Service providers: Stripe (payments), Supabase (database/storage), Expo (push notifications), Sentry (crash reporting), and email delivery providers. Each provider processes data only as instructed and under a data processing agreement.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by valid legal process (court order, subpoena), to protect the rights or safety of Surve or its users, or to prevent fraud.
  • Business transfers: If Surve is acquired or merges, personal data may transfer as a business asset; we will notify affected users.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • Deleted accounts: Upon verified deletion request, personal identifiers are erased within 30 days. Aggregate, anonymised analytics may be retained indefinitely.
  • Financial records: Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
  • Messages: Retained while the booking is active plus 12 months after completion for dispute resolution, then purged or anonymised.
  • Crash logs: Retained for 90 days in Sentry.

10. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt via Supabase Auth; we never store plaintext credentials.
  • Database Row-Level Security policies enforce user-level data isolation.
  • Supabase service-role keys are never exposed to client-side code.
  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint) for sensitive in-app actions.
  • Regular dependency audits and automated vulnerability scanning.

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@surve.app.

11. Cookies & Tracking

The Surve mobile app does not use browser cookies. Session tokens are stored securely in the device's encrypted keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) via expo-secure-store.

The Surve website (surve.app) may use essential cookies for session management and analytics. No third-party advertising cookies are used. A cookie banner will appear on first visit where required by law.

We do not participate in cross-app tracking networks. The iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown as required; if you decline, no tracking identifier is collected.

12. Children's Privacy

Surve is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has registered, we will delete that account promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please email privacy@surve.app.

13. Your Rights & Deletion

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information via Profile → Settings, or by contacting us.
  • Deletion ("right to be forgotten"): Delete your account and all associated personal data. Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account, or email privacy@surve.app. We will process verified deletion requests within 30 days. Note: financial records required by law are retained as described in Section 9.
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV) from Profile → Settings → Account → Export My Data.
  • Objection / Restriction: Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent (e.g., marketing emails), withdraw at any time via your notification preferences or the unsubscribe link in any email.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@surve.app with "Privacy Request" in the subject line and verify your identity. We will respond within 30 days.

15. California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: Request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected, the sources, our business purposes, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to certain exceptions).
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information. If this changes, we will update this policy and provide an opt-out mechanism.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@surve.app or write to us at the address in Section 18. We will verify your identity before fulfilling any request.
